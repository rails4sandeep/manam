Mana Australia Telugu Association

Representing Andhra Pradesh in Australia MATA is formed in 2014 after Andhra Pradesh evolved as new state with an old name. Our motivation is to support everyone associated with Andhra Pradesh in every possible way. We put our best efforts to integrate culture from our home land with Australian culture. We strive to help community and other local communities to understand different life styles associated and develop harmony within. MATA on its part contribute to help local communities in various aspects. MATA conducted Yoga Seminars which helps to promote healthy life style. MATA conducts IT Seminar every year where successful Subject Matter Experts from various domains run a workshop with information of emerging technologies & associated career opportunities MATA takes every opportunity to organize cultural & spiritual events. This will play key role in younger generation of Telugu community getting introduced to various cultural practices. There by ensuring legacy is carried on to future generations
